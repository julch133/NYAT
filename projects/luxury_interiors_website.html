Contractor Website for Luxury Interiors and Remodeling

A professional, mobile-friendly website built to showcase remodeling services, support local search visibility, and make it easier for customers to request work.

Project Overview

Luxury Interiors and Remodeling needed a stronger online presence that clearly explained their services, built customer trust, and gave potential clients an easy way to get in touch.

The website was built with a clean structure, mobile-friendly layout, organized service content, and local search visibility in mind. The goal was to create a professional online home for the business that could support both word-of-mouth referrals and new customer discovery.

Website Preview

The Business Challenge

A contractor website needs to do more than look nice. It has to quickly answer the questions customers care about before they invite someone into their home:

  • What services does the business offer?
  • What areas does the business serve?
  • Can customers trust the company with their home?
  • How can someone request a quote or start a conversation?

Without clear service content, local search structure, and an easy contact path, potential customers may leave before reaching out. The site needed to be simple for visitors while still giving the business room to grow its online visibility over time.

The Solution

The finished site uses a clear service structure, responsive design, project-focused visuals, and simple contact paths to help customers understand the business and take the next step. Local search planning was also included so the website can better support searches for remodeling services in the areas the company serves.

Project Scope

  • Responsive contractor website
  • Service-focused page structure
  • Local search content planning
  • Mobile-friendly layout
  • Project and photo presentation sections
  • Customer contact path
  • Sitemap and robots.txt setup

Technology & Setup

HTML CSS Bootstrap Cloudflare Pages Google Apps Script Local SEO Planning Sitemap Setup robots.txt

Business Value

The website gives Luxury Interiors and Remodeling a professional place to send customers, explain services, display work, and capture project inquiries. It also creates a stronger foundation for local search visibility instead of relying only on word-of-mouth, social media, or direct referrals.
