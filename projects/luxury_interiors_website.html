Luxury Interiors and Remodeling Website

A responsive contractor website built to showcase remodeling services, improve local visibility, and make it easier for customers to request work.

Overview

Luxury Interiors and Remodeling needed a stronger online presence that clearly explained what the business offers and helped potential customers feel confident reaching out. The site was designed to present the company as professional, local, and easy to contact while supporting long-term search visibility for remodeling services.

The project focused on a clean website structure, organized service content, customer trust, and a practical foundation for local SEO.

Website Preview

The Problem

The business needed more than a basic homepage. A contractor website has to quickly answer the questions customers ask before inviting someone into their home:

  • What services do you offer?
  • Where do you work?
  • Can customers trust you with their home?
  • How does someone request a quote or get in touch?

Without clear service pages, local search structure, and an easy contact path, potential customers can leave before reaching out. The site also needed room to grow its online visibility without overloading the main page.

The Solution

I built a mobile-friendly website with organized service sections, project-focused content, contact information, and local SEO structure. The site was designed to make the business look established while keeping the customer path simple, from learning about services to requesting work.

What I Built

  • Responsive contractor website
  • Service sections for remodeling work
  • Local SEO page structure
  • Sitemap and robots.txt support
  • Mobile-friendly layout
  • Contact information and lead path
  • Project and photo presentation structure

Tools Used

HTML CSS Bootstrap Cloudflare Pages Google Apps Script Local SEO Planning Sitemap Setup robots.txt

Business Value

The finished website gives the business a stronger professional presence and a better foundation for getting found online. Instead of relying only on word-of-mouth or social media, the company now has a central place to send customers, explain services, and capture leads.
