Digital Business Launch & Brand Development

Building a technology consulting business from the ground up through branding, website development, SEO, and digital infrastructure.

Project Overview

This project covered the complete lifecycle of launching Not Your Average Tech as a professional technology consulting business.

The work extended beyond creating a website. It included defining the business identity, organizing services, developing the brand, building a digital presence, and establishing the infrastructure needed to attract and support future clients.

The Business Challenge

Launching a new service-based business requires multiple systems and customer touchpoints to work together. The brand, website, services, search presence, marketing materials, and contact processes all needed to communicate a clear and consistent identity.

The objective was to create a complete business ecosystem that could launch professionally while remaining flexible enough to support future growth.

  • Develop a recognizable and credible brand identity
  • Clearly define and organize consulting services
  • Create a professional online presence
  • Build an effective path for prospective clients to make contact
  • Establish a foundation for search visibility
  • Prepare the business for future services and portfolio growth

The Solution

The project encompassed the planning, design, and implementation of the company’s complete digital presence. This included a responsive website, structured service offerings, search optimization, business branding, lead-generation tools, and supporting marketing materials.

Every element was designed to work as part of one connected customer experience while supporting long-term growth and future service expansion.
