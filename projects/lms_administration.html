LMS Administration & Training Lifecycle Management

Supporting learning operations, compliance tracking, and workforce development across a multi-location organization.

Project Overview

This project involved providing administrative and operational support for an organizational learning environment serving employees across 48 locations.

The work covered the full employee training lifecycle—from onboarding and initial course assignments to ongoing development, compliance monitoring, reporting, troubleshooting, and continuous process improvement.

The Business Challenge

Managing employee training at scale requires more than assigning courses and tracking completions. Employees must receive the correct training, compliance requirements must remain visible, and leadership needs dependable reporting.

As the organization continued to grow, its learning and development processes required ongoing administration, technical support, data validation, and process oversight.

  • Deliver accurate training assignments to employees
  • Support onboarding and workforce-development initiatives
  • Monitor compliance-related training requirements
  • Resolve employee access and technical issues
  • Provide leadership with reliable completion reporting
  • Maintain an accessible experience across all locations

The Solution

Provided administrative and operational support for the organization’s Learning Management System, helping manage employee learning from onboarding through ongoing development and compliance training.

  • LMS user administration and account management
  • Training assignment management
  • Learning path administration
  • Compliance tracking and monitoring
  • Reporting and data validation
  • Employee troubleshooting and technical support
  • Documentation development
  • System testing and issue resolution

Operational Improvement Initiatives

LMS Lifecycle Mapping

Developed a comprehensive analysis documenting how employees interacted with learning systems from onboarding through ongoing development. The analysis identified opportunities to improve training delivery, system utilization, and the overall user experience.

Cybersecurity Training Audit

Reviewed cybersecurity training assignments to evaluate completion status, assignment accuracy, and overall coverage. The audit improved visibility into compliance requirements and supported workforce readiness.

Help Desk Workflow Analysis

Evaluated how employee training and LMS support requests were received, handled, and escalated. The analysis identified ways to improve response efficiency and reduce recurring issues.

User Support Documentation

Developed reference materials to help employees complete common LMS tasks and resolve routine issues. These resources improved access to guidance while reducing reliance on direct support.

Training Lifecycle Support

1. Onboarding

Supported new-user setup, initial access, and required training assignments.

2. Development

Administered learning paths and ongoing workforce-development initiatives.

3. Compliance

Monitored completion, assignment accuracy, and compliance training coverage.

4. Reporting

Validated learning data and provided stakeholders with clearer operational visibility.

A well-managed learning environment improves onboarding effectiveness, increases operational consistency, strengthens compliance programs, and supports long-term workforce development.
